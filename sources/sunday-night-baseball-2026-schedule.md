# Full 2026 Schedule for MLB Games on NBC & Peacock

**Author:** Trent Moore | **Published:** Mar 9, 2026 | **Source:** NBC Insider

## Summary

The complete 2026 MLB broadcast schedule for NBC and Peacock, marking the return of baseball to NBC with the debut of Sunday Night Baseball in primetime.

## Key Points

- MLB returns to NBC and Peacock in 2026 with **Sunday Night Baseball** in primetime
- **27 primetime games** across Sunday Night Baseball and special events
- **34 afternoon games**, including 18 MLB Sunday Leadoff games on Peacock
- A Sunday afternoon **whip-around show** on Peacock
- NBC will have exclusive **Wild Card round** coverage
- "**Star-Spangled Sunday**" on July 5 features all 15 MLB games exclusively on NBC/Peacock
- By adding MLB, NBC now has **year-round primetime sports on Sundays** (Sunday Night Football, Sunday Night Basketball, Sunday Night Baseball)

## 2025 World Series Result

- The **Los Angeles Dodgers** defeated the **Toronto Blue Jays** 5–4 in Game 7 of the 2025 World Series (at Rogers Centre, Nov 2, 2025)
- 2025 NL Cy Young Award winner: **Paul Skenes** (Pittsburgh Pirates)

## Opening Day — March 26, 2026

- 1 p.m. ET — Pittsburgh Pirates at New York Mets (NBC/Peacock)
- 8 p.m. ET — Arizona Diamondbacks at Los Angeles Dodgers (NBC/Peacock) — banner-raising game

## Sunday Night Baseball Debut — March 29, 2026

- 7 p.m. ET — Cleveland Guardians at Seattle Mariners (Peacock)

## Notable Division Rivalries Featured

- Diamondbacks–Dodgers (Mar 26), Braves–Phillies (Apr 19), Tigers–Royals (May 10)
- Rangers–Angels (May 24), Cubs–Cardinals (May 31), Mets–Phillies (Jun 21)
- Yankees–Red Sox (Jun 28), Padres–Dodgers (Jul 5), Mariners–Astros (Aug 16), Padres–Giants (Sep 13)
