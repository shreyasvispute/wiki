## Roster & Service Rules

| Rule | Number | Detail |
|------|-------:|--------|
| 40-man roster | 40 | Required for MLB active roster eligibility |
| Domestic Reserve List cap | 165 | In-season; all org-controlled players |
| MLB service year | 172 days | On the active roster; drives free agency/arbitration |
| Rule 5 protection (signed ≤18) | 5 seasons | Must add to 40-man or risk exposure |
| Rule 5 protection (signed 19+) | 4 seasons | Must add to 40-man or risk exposure |
| Active roster (Triple-A) | ~28 | Approximate active roster size |

## Player Development Timelines

| Background | Typical Path | Years to MLB |
|-----------|-------------|:------------:|
| College draftee (fast track) | High-A → Double-A → Triple-A → MLB | 1–3 |
| U.S. high school draftee | Complex → Low-A → High-A → AA → AAA → MLB | 3–7+ |
| International teen signing | DSL → Complex → Low-A → High-A → AA → AAA → MLB | 3–7+ |
